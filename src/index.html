TrackMe Wearable Devices

TrackMe develops health-monitoring devices devoted to measure and record different parameters related to the health status of the person (body temperature, heart rate, blood pressure and percentage of O2 in the blood) and also the location.

TrackMe Services

TrackMe offers various services today, such as, Data4Help, AutomatedSOS and Track4Run. Click on the services to know more.

Different services to suit every need!

Data4Help

A system capable to store the physical health data of any individual using any TrackMe wearable device

AutomatedSOS

A system that will be built on top of Data4Help, and will send requests to every elderly individual in order to access its physical health parameters

View Details

Track4Run

A system built on top of Data4Help, will send requests to every user to access location and let them participate in a defined running circuit
